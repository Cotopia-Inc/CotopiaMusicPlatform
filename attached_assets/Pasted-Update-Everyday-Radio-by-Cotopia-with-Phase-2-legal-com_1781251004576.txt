PLATFORM BRANDING

Everyday Radio by Cotopia

Short display name:

Everyday Radio

Brand line:

Powered by Cotopia

Cotopia is the parent company and platform operator. Cotopia Music & Marketing is the record label division. Everyday Radio is a separate independent streaming, hosting, promotion, and community platform powered by Cotopia.

BUSINESS MODEL

Everyday Radio is not currently a royalty-paying streaming service.

It is a platform license, content hosting, streaming, discovery, promotion, and community platform.

Users keep ownership of their content. Users grant Cotopia a limited license to host, stream, display, promote, feature, recommend, and place their content into playlists.

Cotopia does not currently pay streaming royalties, mechanical royalties, performance royalties, publishing royalties, or revenue sharing unless a separate written agreement exists.

Cotopia may launch optional creator compensation, contests, sponsorships, revenue sharing, or partner programs later.

SUBMISSION CHECKBOXES

Before payment or final submission, users must check required legal boxes:

1. I own or control all rights necessary to upload this content.
2. I grant Cotopia a non-exclusive license to host, stream, display, promote, feature, recommend, and place this content in playlists.
3. I understand Everyday Radio by Cotopia does not currently pay streaming royalties, mechanical royalties, performance royalties, publishing royalties, or revenue sharing unless covered by a separate written agreement.
4. I understand submission and promotion fees are non-refundable once review begins.
5. I understand Cotopia may remove content, suspend accounts, or reject submissions that violate platform rules.
6. If AI was used, I confirm I have the rights necessary to upload and distribute the content.
7. I agree to defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Cotopia and its related entities if my upload causes legal claims.

Users cannot submit or pay until all required checkboxes are accepted.

AI POLICY

Everyday Radio accepts AI-generated and AI-assisted music, videos, artwork, and creative content.

Cotopia does not train AI systems using user-uploaded content.

Users must have rights to upload AI-generated or AI-assisted content.

Prohibit users from uploading AI content that copies, impersonates, or infringes another artist, label, voice, likeness, song, beat, or copyrighted work.
