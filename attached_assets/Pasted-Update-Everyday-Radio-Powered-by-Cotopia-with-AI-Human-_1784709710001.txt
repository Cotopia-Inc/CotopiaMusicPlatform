2. CREATOR TAG CONTROLS

On content upload and content settings, allow creators to select:

* Human Created
* AI Assisted
* Hybrid Human + AI
* Fully AI Generated

Explain each option clearly.

Human Created:
No generative AI was used to create the expressive music, lyrics, vocals, composition, performance, or video content. Routine tools such as cleanup, noise reduction, or conventional editing may be disclosed separately.

AI Assisted:
AI was used as a tool, but the creator supplied meaningful human authorship, performance, arrangement, editing, or transformation.

Hybrid Human + AI:
Both human-authored and AI-generated elements materially contributed to the finished work.

Fully AI Generated:
The expressive content was generated entirely or substantially entirely by AI without meaningful human creative authorship.

If the creator selects Fully AI Generated:

* Show the platform policy
* Warn that Everyday Radio currently does not accept fully AI-generated work
* Allow the record to be saved as a rejected or draft submission for audit purposes
* Do not publish it
* Do not charge a submission fee
* Do not delete the record

4. TAG PRIORITY AND LOCKING

Use this priority order:

1. Final appeal decision
2. Admin-assigned tag
3. Moderator flag pending admin review
4. Creator-selected tag
5. Detection-system suggestion

Creator-selected tags:

* Creator may tag or untag their own content
* Creator may change Human, AI Assisted, or Hybrid classification
* Changes must be logged
* Material changes may trigger a new moderator review

Admin-assigned tags:

* Admin and master_admin may assign, change, or remove content-origin tags
* When admin assigns a tag, set tag_locked = true
* Creator cannot remove or change a locked tag
* Show a message: “This classification was assigned by Everyday Radio and is locked pending administrative review or appeal.”
* Admin can unlock the tag
* Every lock, unlock, tag, and untag action must create an audit-log entry

Moderator actions:

* Moderators cannot make a permanent public classification
* Moderators may flag suspected misclassification
* Moderators may recommend Human, AI Assisted, Hybrid, or Fully AI Generated
* Moderator flags send the submission to admin final review
* Moderator may add evidence notes and detection results
* Moderator cannot lock a creator’s tag permanently
* Moderator cannot make the final appeal decision

9. AUTOMATIC FULLY-AI REJECTION

Add a master-admin-controlled feature flag:

auto_reject_fully_ai_content

When disabled:

* Detection results only flag content for human review

When enabled:

Auto-rejection may occur only when:

* The creator self-declares Fully AI Generated; or
* The configured detection result meets the master-admin threshold and the policy explicitly permits automatic rejection

Recommended safer default:

* Automatically reject only creator-declared Fully AI Generated content
* Route detector-flagged content to moderator/admin review rather than automatic rejection

If detector-based auto-rejection is enabled:

* Preserve the submission
* Do not publish
* Do not charge or retain a submission fee unless the refund policy clearly allows it
* Provide the reason
* Provide a direct appeal link
* Allow admin override
* Log the model/version, score, threshold, and decision
* Never permanently ban a user based only on detector output
