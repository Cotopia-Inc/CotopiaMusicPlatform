# Our Promise

## Welcome to Everyday Radio by Cotopia

Everyday Radio was built because too many creators feel overlooked, unheard, restricted, or pushed aside.

We believe great music, videos, stories, interviews, and creative works can come from anyone, anywhere.

Whether you're an independent artist, an emerging label, a content creator, an AI creator, a producer, a filmmaker, or simply someone with a vision, you deserve a place to be discovered.

## What We Believe

We believe creators should have opportunities.

We believe independent artists matter.

We believe communities matter.

We believe discovery should be open.

We believe technology should empower creators, not replace them.

We believe AI can be a creative tool when used responsibly and legally.

We believe music and media should bring people together.

## What Everyday Radio Is

Everyday Radio is a platform for:

* Music
* Videos
* Creative media
* Discovery
* Community
* Conversation
* Promotion
* Growth

Every song can have a community.

Every creator can build an audience.

Every listener can discover something new.

## What Makes Us Different

We are not focused on gatekeepers.

We are not focused on industry politics.

We are not focused on limiting creativity.

We are focused on providing tools, visibility, opportunity, and community.

We support original creators.

We support AI creators.

We support independent artists.

We support independent labels.

We support people building something from the ground up.

## Ownership Matters

Creators keep ownership of their work.

We believe creators should understand their rights and responsibilities.

We believe transparency is important.

We believe trust is earned.

## Community Comes First

Everyday Radio is more than a streaming platform.

It is a community.

We encourage conversation, collaboration, discovery, and mutual respect.

Every user helps shape the future of the platform.

## Innovation Matters

Technology changes.

Media changes.

Creativity evolves.

Everyday Radio is committed to adapting, improving, and exploring new technologies while supporting creators and protecting the integrity of the platform.

## Our Commitment

We will continue building tools that help creators grow.

We will continue listening to our community.

We will continue creating opportunities for discovery.

We will continue supporting independent voices.

And we will continue working to make Everyday Radio a place where creators, listeners, and communities can thrive together.

Thank you for being part of the journey.
